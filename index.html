Services

People say that we are crazy and should start selling our prints, well here is what we say for those people... You are a banana!
You already know that we give prints away without asking for money (if you didn't now you do) and here is how it works:

1- Tell us your story

Tell us a bit more about yourself before we offer you a print, if you are rich... why? If you are poor, ok. If you are not a banana OMG TAKE ALL OF IT!

2- Ask politely

We all have conscious and are easily psychologically hurt, if you say pwease ofc we will give you, if you say f*** you , well f*** you 2.

3- Request details

For us it is easier to know what exactly you want, like the color, size, quality or other details. If you don't know, well we don't know as well.

4- Module link

It is optional, but if you have the module link well then its way less work for us and you will receive the thing faster, if not, uhhh

5- Wait

Be patient, remember everyone likes free stuff so there may be more in line, also if you live outside Portugal, oh... extra payment for us...

6- Review

Tell us what you think, what you liked and disliked or if there is something wrong etc... If it is bad we can make another one to compensate.

7- Share

Share online, facebook or linkdin or others, friends, share a like, subscribe hit the bell icon, donate to our patreon, support our kickstarter, send us gifts, mbway all the way, share the coffee, share sum luv. This is all option you don't need say anything after you receive the print and we will just be a lil extra sad, no worries. *sniff*
